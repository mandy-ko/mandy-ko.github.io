Introduction

Threads & Needles

Threads & Needles is a digital magazine featuring embroidery artwork and projects created by small artists. The magazine includes articles about embroidery tools, stitching techniques, and workshops/tutorials for readers of all skill levels. There will also be embroidery patterns available for the reader to download and use.
