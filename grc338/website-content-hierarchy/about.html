About Us

  • Specialties

    Fresh, local ingredients. Recipes carefully crafted for optimal flavor. That's what a meal at The Breakfast Club is all about. Well, that and having a great time. When you mix our good vibes with our great food, you're in for a delicious treat. We've got all your favorite sweet dishes in our lineup -- waffles, pancakes, French toast. Or, for our savory food fanatics, we have burgers, sandwiches, and Benedicts. It's time you did brunch The Breakfast Club way.

  • History

    The Breakfast Club opened its doors in 2016. We serve breakfast, lunch, and brunch dishes -- all day long. Your taste buds will be able to celebrate our more than 30 years of family cooking expertise with our signature breakfast dishes and lunch items.
